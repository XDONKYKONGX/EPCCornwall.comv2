About Us

Cornwall's Leading EPC Provider

With years of experience serving Cornwall, we provide professional, efficient EPC assessments you can trust.

EPC Cornwall is a locally owned and operated business specializing in Energy Performance Certificates for all property types. Our team of fully accredited assessors are committed to delivering excellent service with a personal touch.

We understand that every property is unique, which is why we take the time to conduct thorough assessments while keeping the process simple and stress-free for our clients.

  • Same-day appointments available
  • Certificates typically issued within 24 hours
  • Serving all of Cornwall and surrounding areas
  • Friendly, professional service
